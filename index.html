Elegy Animation Portfolio

Elegy is a multi-disciplinary artist specializing in low-poly 3D animation.

Most of his 3D work can be seen alonside his music project of the same name, where he utilizes a digital avatar as his mascot.

DELETE

Seamless loop implemented with full city design, moving car animation, and glow/bloom effects.

BOYS LIKE ME

"Mini-world" implemented with active animations, low-poly fire particle system, and camera movement.

SILHOUETTE

Short loop using running character animation, particle system on disappearing floor, and character design.
